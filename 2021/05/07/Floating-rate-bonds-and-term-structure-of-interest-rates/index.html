Floating rate bonds and term structure of interest rates

Posted on 2021-05-07 In RMFE Views:

Linear pricing(线性定价)

Theorem (Linear Pricing) Suppose there is no arbitrage. Suppose also

  • Price of cash flow \(C _{A}\) is \(p_{A}\)
  • Price of cash flow \(c _{B}\) is \(p_{B}\)

Then the price of cash flow that pays \(c = c _{A}+ c _{B}\) must be \(p_{A}+p_{B}\).

不满足改定理的定价策略必然会形成套利

Floating interest rates(浮动利率)

实际上,利率是一个随机量,随着时间而波动.

作出以下定义 - Let \(r_{k}\) denote the per period interest rate over period \([k, k+1)\) - The exact value of \(r_{k}\) becomes known only at time \(k\) - 1-period loans issued in period \(k\) to be repaid in period \(k+1\) are charged \(r_{k}\) - Cash flow of floating rate bond - coupon payment at time \(k: r_{k-1} F\)(使用上一期的利率) - face value at time \(n: F\) - Goal: Compute the arbitrage-free price \(P_{f}\) of the floating rate bond - Split up the cash flows of floating rate bond into simpler cash flows - \(p_{k}=\) Price of contract paying \(r_{k-1} F\) at time \(k\) - \(P=\) Price of Principal \(F\) at time \(n=\frac{F}{(1+r)^{n}}\) Price of floating rate bond \(P_{f}=P+\sum_{k=1}^{n} p_{k}\) #### Price of contract that pays \(r_{k-1} F\) at time \(k\) Cash flow at time \(k\) \[ \begin{aligned} c_{k} &=r_{k-1} F-\alpha\left(1+r_{0}\right)^{k-1}\left(1+r_{k-1}\right)+\alpha\left(1+r_{0}\right)^{k} \\ &=\underbrace{\left(F-\alpha\left(1+r_{0}\right)^{k-1}\right) r_{k-1}}_{\text {random }}+\underbrace{\alpha r_{0}\left(1+r_{0}\right)^{k-1}}_{\text {deterministic }} \end{aligned} \] Set \(\alpha=\frac{F}{\left(1+r_{0}\right)^{(k-1)}}\). Then the random term is \(0 .\) Net cash flow is now deterministic \(\ldots c_{k}=\alpha r_{0}\left(1+r_{0}\right)^{k-1}=F r_{0}\)

Price of floating rate bond

\[ \begin{aligned} P_{f} &=\frac{F}{\left(1+r_{0}\right)^{n}}+\sum_{k=1}^{n} p_{k} \\ &=\frac{F}{\left(1+r_{0}\right)^{n}}+\sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{F r_{0}}{\left(1+r_{0}\right)^{k}} \\ &=\frac{F}{\left(1+r_{0}\right)^{n}}+\frac{F r_{0}}{\left(1+r_{0}\right)} \sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{1}{\left(1+r_{0}\right)^{k-1}} \\ &=\frac{F}{\left(1+r_{0}\right)^{n}}+\frac{F r_{0}}{\left(1+r_{0}\right)} \cdot \frac{1-\frac{1}{\left(1+r_{0}\right)^{n}}}{1-\frac{1}{1+r_{0}}} \\ &=F \end{aligned} \] The price \(P_{f}\) of a floating rate bond is equal to its face value \(F\)

浮动利率的情况下,无套利的价格为面值
